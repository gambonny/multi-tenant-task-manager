1. Assuming Cloudflare manages DNS for the domain, I would configure the DKIM and DMARC TXT records provided by the email provider (e.g., Resend) in Cloudflare’s DNS dashboard. DKIM allows receiving mail servers to verify the email was authorized by the domain, while DMARC defines how to handle messages that fail authentication checks. This protects the domain from spoofing and improves deliverability.
